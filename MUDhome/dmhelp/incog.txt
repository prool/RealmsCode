Help on DM command:
	INCOG

Toggles the incognito flag. When set, you will be visibile
only to those in the same room. Any global commands (broadcast, tell)
will identify the staff member, but players will not be able to
detect him (who, whois, tell).

*incog and incog are the same command.

: *incog
You cloak your presence.
: incog
You unlock your pressence.
