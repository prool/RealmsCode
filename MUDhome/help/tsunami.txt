MP cost: Dependant on the number of targets.

This spell affects all monsters in the area that the spell is cast in. Summoning
another version of the hurricane, one more water-based and somtimes even more
brutal, the caster is able to direct the torrent caused by the tsunami to any
targets in the room, damaging them all. This spell is part of the water realm.

Type "help aoe" for more information.

Syntax:
cast tsunami [<monsters>|<players>|<all>]
