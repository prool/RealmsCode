MP cost: 4
Shortcut: m

Mend-wounds is a powerful healing spell. Wounds suddenly begin to repair
themselves and soon all disappear. More pious casters will often experience
more benifit from this spell. Thus, paladins or clerics who have prayed will
often experience the most healing.

Syntax:
cast mend <target>
