MP cost: 10

This spell removes poison from the its target, whether delivered by magic of
physical means. Thus, poison contracted from any source is able to be lifted by
this spell. Only clerics and paladins may cast this spell, but others may cast
a lesser version called slow-poison.

Syntax:
cast cure-poison <target>
