Vampires may hypnotize opponents in order to allow them to not be attacked for
the duration of the hypnosis. Players that are hypnotized may not attack a
player who has previously hypnotized him while it lasts. It will not work
against permanent monsters.

Example:
: hyp p
You hypnotize the pickpocket.
