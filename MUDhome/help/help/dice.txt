This command allows you to roll a combination of dice you specify. If no
combination of dice is specified, it is assumed 2 6-sided dice are rolled (2d6).
This is useful for things such as dividing up treasure amongst a group, or just
sitting around and gambling when idling.

Example:
: dice 2d6+2
You roll 2d6+2
: 11
