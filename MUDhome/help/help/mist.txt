Vampires may mist in order to disappear from sight. They cannot be seen until
they attack a monster or player. This is especially good for surprising players
for the kill. You may only mist during the night. A true-sight spell will allow
you to see misted vampires.

: mist
You turn to mist.
: The sun rises.
: You return to your natural form.
