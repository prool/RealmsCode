This command allows you to see what language you are currently speaking, as well
as the languages you currently know how to speak and understand.

Example:
: lang
Currently speaking: common.
Languages known: elven, common, orcish, giantkin, darkelf, goblinoid, celestial,
  infernal, wolfen, thieves' cant.
