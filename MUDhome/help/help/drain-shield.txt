MP cost: 15

When cast, this spell renders the target resistant to exp drain by monsters,
drain from player liches, and also bite from player vampires. Clerics and
paladins cast this spell much better then other classes. The duration of the
cast is longer from them then any other class. This spell can be cast on
monsters as well.

Syntax:
cast drain-shield <target>
