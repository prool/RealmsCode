Clerics of Enoch upon reaching a high enough level are able to pronounce a
holyword on their evil opponents. This is able to either do extensive damage to
their opponent or destroy them utterly.

Example:
: holy mug
Your holy word utterly destroys the mugger.
You killed the mugger.
You gained 30 experience for the death of the mugger.
