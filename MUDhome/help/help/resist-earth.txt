MP cost: 13

This spell halves all magical spell damage from the earth realm. An intangible
shield of earth forms around the target, attempting to nullify and offensive
magic that comes toward it and the target. Note that only magic from the realm
of earth is affected.

See "help resist" for more information.

Syntax:
cast resist-earth <target>
