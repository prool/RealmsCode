Staff Heirarchy and Explanations


Dungeonmaster

Overlords of the game in all aspects. Have final word on all policy decisions.
Enforce policy and security of the mud, write code, build areas, items, and
creatures, and have access to the shell. Have unrestricted access to the entire
mud. They are the final point of contact for players, and are unable to be
mailed directly.


Caretakers

Chief enforcers of the policy. Build new areas, creatures, and items. Have
unrestricted access to the entire mud. Supervises builders. Second to the last
point of contact for players. By default they are unable to be mailed by
players, however, they may act as a Watcher if they so choose (see below).


Builder

Primary builders of the mud. Build new areas and creatures. Limited access to
specific areas, set by Dungeonmasters. No contact with players possible - always
invisible to players.


Watcher

Veteran players in service of Realms, acting as liasons. Assists players and
contacts Caretakers or Dungeonmasters if needed. A list of all watchers can be
found by typing "who watchers" at the prompt. If you need any kind of
assistance, please mudmail them (HELP MUDMAIL) or talk to them.
