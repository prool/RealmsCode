MP cost: Dependant on the number of targets.

This spell affects all monsters in the area that the spell is cast in. A small
yet powerful earthquake is temporarily summoned to the area after the spell is
attacked, using its power to assault all the creatures designated. It is part
of the earth realm.

Type "help aoe" for more information.

Syntax:
cast earthquake [<monsters>|<players>|<all>]
