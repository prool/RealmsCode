This command allows you to buy items from monsters, providing they have
something to sell.  To find out what a monster may have for sale, use the
SELECTION command.

Example:
: purch wood kor
You pay Korben the Instructor 75 gold pieces.
Korben the Instructor says, "Thank you very much.  Here is your wooden key."
