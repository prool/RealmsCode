MP cost: variable

This spell causes the target to be silenced and not able to speak, emote, send,
classsend, or clansend, broadcast, or cast spells for its duration. This spell
can be cast on others only 3x a day, and it also is unable to be cast on another
player in the middle of combat with a monster. The MP cost is lower for clerics
and  higher for other classes that can cast it.

Syntax:
c silence <target>
