Ambush is an ability only availaible to rogues. It essentially allows a rogue
to attack from hiding with both weapons, making both weapons do half-again as
much damage. Ambush can only be done with sharp, thrust, and blunt weapons,
and BOTH hands must hold a weapon for it to work.

Syntax:
: ambush wino
You ambush the wino!
You bludgeoned the wino for 7 damage.
You attack the wino!
You bludgeoned the wino for 6 damage.
You killed the wino.
You gained 6 experience for the death of the wino.
