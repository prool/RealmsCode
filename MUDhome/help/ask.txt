Talk allows you to communicate with certain monsters. Some monsters can even
carry on interactive conversations. If you supply a keyword, the monster may be
able to give you some more information about it.

Syntax:
  ask <monster> [#] [<keyword>]
  parley <monster> [#] [<keyword>]
  talk <monster> [#] [<keyword>]

Example:
: talk x
Xenis the Sage says to you, "What do you need to know about?"
: talk x training
Xenis the Sage says to you, "Oh. Training is a must! What is your profession?"
