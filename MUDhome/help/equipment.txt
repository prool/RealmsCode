Equipment shows you a list of the objects that you are currently wearing,
wielding and holding.

Example:
: eq
On body:   some cured leather armor
On hands:  some hard leather gloves
On head:   a black leather skullcap
On waist:  an iron-studded leather belt
On face:   a hard leather mask
