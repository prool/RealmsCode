This is the level three spell of the air realm. It causes a massive whirlwind to
hit the target, picking up anything nearby and striking the target with it.
Whirlwind also pushes the target around and often into heavy and pointy objects,
causing even more damage.

Syntax:
cast whirlwind <target>
