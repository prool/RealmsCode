This command, if done successfully, allows a death knight to exceed his or her
maximum HP. They can surpass the maximum by the energy their evil arua drains
off of their opponent, which gets added on to their HP.

Example:
(32 H 11 M): bloodsac
Your blood loss feeds the demons you serve.  You feel their power surround you.
(32 H 11 M): k worker
You slash the field worker for 5 damage.
Your aura of evil drains 2 hit points from your opponent.
