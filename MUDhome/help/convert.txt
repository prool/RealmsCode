Convert will allow you to convert yourself from chaotic to lawful alignment on
your own without the aid of a DM. You will be prompted to be sure you wish to do
this. Realize that once you convert, you may never go back to being chaotic ever
again. Type HELP ALIGNMENT for more information on alignment.

Example:
: convert
To prevent accidental conversion you must confirm you want to convert,
to do so type 'convert yes'
Remember, you will NEVER be able to be chaotic again.
: convert yes
### Bob just converted to lawful alignment.
