Sign is a command useable only by dark elf players. Dark elves, through rigorous
training through their young lives, are able to speak their language just as
easily in sign language as they do speaking normally. You may sign to any other
dark elf in the current room. Any other dark elves in the room will also
comprehend what you signed as well. Note that being blind will prevent people
from understanding you when you sign to them.

Example:
: sign bob Attack!
You signed, "Attack!" to Bob.
