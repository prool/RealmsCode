The touch of death is a ability monks gain once they reach a level of 10. It
allows them to do damage beyond that of a normal hit and sometimes may fatally
wound a opponent to death.

Example:
: touch win
You fatally wound the wino.
You killed the wino.
You gained 6 experience for the death of the wino.
