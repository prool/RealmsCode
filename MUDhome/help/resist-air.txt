This spell halves all magical spell damage from the air realm. An intangible
shield of air forms around the target, attempting to nullify and offensive magic
that comes toward it and the target. Note that only magic from the realm of air
is affected.

See "help resist" for more information.

Syntax:
cast resist-air <target>
