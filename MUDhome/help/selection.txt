This command allows you to see what a given monster has for sale. It will only
work with certain monsters. After you have a selection from the monster, you may
use the PURCHASE or BUY command to buy one of the items.

You may also use the list command to see what a monster is selling.

Example:
: select i
Korben the Instructor is currently selling:
1) wooden key                75gp
2) steel key                 150gp
3) bronze key                300gp
