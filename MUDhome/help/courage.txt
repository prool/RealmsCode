MP cost: 20

This spell imbues the target with an unnatural courage. As such, they are immune
to magical fears of any kind. If already under magical fear, this spell will
neutralize such fear. This spell is only able to be cast by paladins and clerics
of a good or martial faith.

Syntax:
cast courgae <target>
