Clerics of Aramon are able to summon undead creatures to come and aid them in
battle. This ability is gained upon pledging to the Cult of Aramon. The higher
your title is, the more powerful the undead you summon will be.

Syntax:
: animate
: A skeletal dog crawls forth from the earth to aid you.
A skeletal dog just arrived.
