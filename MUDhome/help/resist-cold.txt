MP cost: 13

This spell halves all magical spell damage from the cold realm.
An intangible shield of cold forms around the target, attempting
to nullify and offensive magic that comes toward it and the
target. Note that only magic from the realm of cold is affected.

See "help resist" for more information.

Syntax:
cast resist-cold <target>
