This command will show you who is having a birthday today. It works similar
to the "who" command in that, if you cannot see the person due to
invisibily or such, you will not be able to tell if they are having a
birthday.

: birthday
It is the 21st of Moitra, the 5th month of the year.
Today is Bob's birthday! He is 18 years old.
