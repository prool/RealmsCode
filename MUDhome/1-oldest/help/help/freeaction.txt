MP cost: 15
School:  Abjuration
Domain:  Protection

This spell allows the target to be freed of any binding or holding magics, such
as hold-person or stun. It also allows the recipient to move freely underwater,
or over hard to move terrain. Only clerics and druids are able to cast this
spell.
