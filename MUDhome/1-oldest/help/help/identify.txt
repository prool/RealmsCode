At higher levels, a bard is able to identify certain aspects of various items in
the game. The item must be in the bards inventory in order to do it.

Example:
: id hazy
You carefully study the hazy potion.
You manage to learn about the hazy potion!
The hazy potion is a potion, with the word-of-recall spell.
It is worth 10000 gold coins, and is brand new.
