MP cost: 10
School:  Evocation
Domain:  Destruction

This spell is similar to lightning-bolt, except that it is a thrown ball of
blue-white energy that shocks the nervous system of the target, doing great
damage. In effect it is an upgrade to the previous spell that will hurt the
target more.

Syntax:
cast shockbolt <target>
