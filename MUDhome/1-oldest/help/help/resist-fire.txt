MP cost: 13
School:  Abjuration
Domain:  Protection

This spell halves all magical spell damage from the fire realm. An intangible
shield of fire forms around the target, attempting to nullify and offensive
magic that comes toward it and the target. Note that only magic from the realm
of fire is affected.

See "help resist" for more information.

Syntax:
cast resist-fire <target>
