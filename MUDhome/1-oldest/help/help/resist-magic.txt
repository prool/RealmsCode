MP cost: 25
School:  Abjuration
Domain:  Not Castable

This spell protects its target from magical attacks. This spell will form a
magical shield around the target that will prevent many spells from affecting
it. However, any benefitial spells that can affect the target still will. Only
mages and liches may cast this spell.

Syntax:
cast resist-magic <target>
