Evocation spells deal with creating energy out of nothing. Their main
focus is combat magic - nearly all evocation spells deal damage to their
targets.

The six elemental realms are considered sub-schools of the school of
evocation. The six realms are:

    Air Realm            Cold Realm
    Earth Realm          Electric Realm
    Fire Realm           Water Realm
