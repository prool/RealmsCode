Remove allows you to remove anything you are wearing, wielding or holding. To
see what you are currently wearing, wielding and holding, type the "equipment"
command. Then you may remove any item that is listed. Typing "remove all" will
remove all equipment that you are currently wearing.

Example:
: rm hoe
You removed a hoe.
