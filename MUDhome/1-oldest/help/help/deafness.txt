MP cost: 30
School:  Transmutation
Domain:  Trickery

The deadfness spell will cause the target to lose all hearing. They will
still be aware of people speaking in the room but will hear nothing. A
sleeping deaf person cannot be awoken by loud noises.

Syntax:
cast deafness <target>
