MP cost: 20
School:  Not Castable
Domain:  Evil

The unhallow spell fills a room with an unholy aura, increasing the damage of
all undead creatures in the room. An unhallow spell can be detected by a
know-aura spell. Hallow cancels the effects of this spell.

Syntax:
cast unhallow
