MP cost: 20
School:  Not Castable
Domain:  Good

The hallow spell fills a room with a holy aura, decreasing the damage of all
undead creatures in the room. A hallow spell can be detected by a know-aura
spell. Unhallow cancels the effects of this spell.

Syntax:
cast hallow
