Vampires may bite players or monsters during the night doing extra damage
than normal by depleting the opponents blood. If the vampire is injured,
they will be partially healed by this bite.

Syntax:
: A wino just arrived.
: bite w
You bite the wino for 36 damage.
The blood of the wino revitalizes your strength.
You killed the wino.
You gained 6 experience for the death of the wino.
