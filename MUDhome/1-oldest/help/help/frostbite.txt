MP cost: 7
School:  Evocation
Domain:  Destruction

This spell causes frost to bite into the target's entire form. The resulting
effects will often include immobile limbs, damaged nerves, and any other
ill-effects of being out in the cold for too long.

Syntax:
cast frostbite <target>
