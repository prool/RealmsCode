MP cost: 30
School:  Transmutation
Domain:  Augmentation

The haste spell increases your dexterity, making you quicker and able to perform
tasks more efficiently. This spell does not last long, but while it is in
effect, you will able to attack every 2 seconds in combat instead of every 3.
A haste spell counteracts the effects of a slow spell.

A haste spell will not work in conjunction with the frenzy ability.
Only a mage, lich, druid, or cleric may cast this spell.

Syntax:
cast haste <target>
