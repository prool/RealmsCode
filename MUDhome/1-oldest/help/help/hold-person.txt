MP cost: 20
School:  Enchantment
Domain:  Trickery

Hold-person is a spell only castable by mages, paladins, clerics, and liches. It
will magically render the victim immobile, however, the target may still fight
back. They are simply prevented from leaving the room for a short time.

Syntax:
cast hold-person <target>
