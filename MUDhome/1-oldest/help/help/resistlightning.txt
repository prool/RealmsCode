MP cost: 13
School:  Abjuration
Domain:  Protection

This spell protects you from spells in the electricity realm, causing you to
take half damage. In addition, it protects you against electricity traps and
lightning breath weapons. Note that only magic from the realm of electricity
is affected.

See "help resist" for more information.

Syntax:
cast resist-lightning <target>
