MP cost: 12
School:  Divination
Domain:  Not Castable

This spell will provide information about the possible future of the target
creature. Fate is generally hard to trust, but luckier targets are more likely
to survive what the future brings. Fortune may only be cast by bards.

Syntax:
cast fortune <target>
