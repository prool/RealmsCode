MP cost: 25
School:  Evocation
Domain:  Destruction

This is the most powerful spell in the air realm. It creates a storm to descend
on the target of this spell, blasting them with the full strength of the most
fearsome storm. The physical damage to the target's body is immense, often high
enough to prove fatal.

Syntax:
cast tempest <target>
