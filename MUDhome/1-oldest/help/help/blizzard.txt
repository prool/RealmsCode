MP cost: Dependant on the number of targets.
School:  Evocation
Domain:  Destruction

This spell affects all monsters in the area that the spell is cast in. The
strong magic from this spell summons a temporary blizzard, bringing down
the elemental forces into the area. Targets suffer frostbite, numbing of
limbs, and a severe hypothermic reaction. This spell is part of the cold
realm.

Type "help aoe" for more information.

Syntax:
cast blizzard [<monsters>|<players>|<all>]
