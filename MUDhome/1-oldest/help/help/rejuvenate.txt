MP cost: 8
School:  Not Castable

This spell is available only to clerics of the goddess CERIS. It is both a
healing spell as well as an MP regeneration spell. It heals a little less than
vigor (HELP VIGOR), but also "heals" some magic points back also.

Syntax:
cast rejuvenate <target>
