MP cost: 8
School:  Translocation
Domain:  Not Castable

Transport allows a caster to send an item to a player. The player must be able
to hold the weight of the item for the transport to work. This spell can be used
to transfer valuable goods across great distances instantaneously. Many
business-involved wizards find this spell very useful in conjunction with the
bank system. This spell is only castable by mages and liches.

Syntax:
cast transport <item> <target>
