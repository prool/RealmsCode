MP cost: 7
School:  Evocation
Domain:  Destruction

This spell causes a forked arc of electricity to spring from the caster's hands,
slamming into the target and electrically burning them. Survivors often
experience nervous-system problems resulting from being the target of this
spell.

Syntax:
cast lightning-bolt <target>
