MP cost: 45
School:  Enchantment
Domain:  Trickery

The globe-of-silence spells fills the entire room with a silence spell,
preventing everyone within the radius from speaking. The caster of this spell
is not immune to the effect.

Syntax:
cast globe-of-silence
