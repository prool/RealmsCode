MP cost: 25
School:  Enchantment
Domain:  Trickery

The scare spell causes brief but intense fear in the target. The target will
be magically compelled to flee the room but will regain their senses
immediately. Creatures immune to fear will not be affected by this spell.

Syntax:
cast scare <target>
