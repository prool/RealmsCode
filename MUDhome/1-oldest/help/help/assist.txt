syntax: assist <player>

This command will assist <player>, setting your target to theirs as long 
as they are in the same room as you.  If they have no target your target
will be cleared.

Ex:
: ^ytar^x
You are targetting: Noone!

: ^yassist Plu^x
You assist Plu!
You are now targeting black rat.
