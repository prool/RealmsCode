MP cost: 10
School:  Abjuration
Domain:  Protection

This spell improves its target's defense skill by surrounding it with a magic
field. This magic will attempt to intercept any blows aimed at the target. Note,
however, that this shield will not prevent any magic damage from getting
through.

Syntax:
cast protection <target>
