MP cost: 10
School:  Abjuration
Domain:  Trickery

This spell nullifies the effects of a fear spell on the target. In effect, the
target experiences a boost of bravery strong enough to overcome magic. Thus, the
target will once again be under their own free will.

Syntax:
cast remove-fear <target>
