MP cost: 20
School:  Transmutation
Domain:  Other

This spell turns solid stone into living flesh. If cast on a character who was
recently petrified, this will reverse the petrification. Type HELP
PETRIFICATION for more details on stoning.

Syntax:
cast stone-to-flesh <target>
