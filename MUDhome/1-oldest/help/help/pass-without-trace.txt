MP cost: 8
Domain:  Nature

This spell allows the target to travel through a room and leave no tracks.
You passage will not disturb any information that can be detected by the track
ability. This spell may only be cast by druids, rangers, and followers of
Linothan.

Syntax:
cast pass-without-trace <target>
