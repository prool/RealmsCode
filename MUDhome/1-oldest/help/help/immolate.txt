MP cost: 16
School:  Evocation
Domain:  Destruction

This is the second most powerful spell in the fire realm. It causes to target to
suddenly combust into a dead ball of flame and will often result in the violent
death of the target.  Major burns are nearly always noted in the victims of this
spell.

Syntax:
cast immolate <target>
