This command allows you to reclaim an item that you recently either sold in a
pawn shop or dropped in a pawn shop. You will be required to return the money
that the shopkeeper paid for the item. You will only be able to do this as
long as you do not leave the room and do not log off. This only works for the
last item that you pawned. ^WNote:^x unique items cannot be reclaimed.

Example:
: sell wh
The shopkeep gives you 125 gold for the white colored sword.
Your haggling skills gained you an extra 47 gold coins.
: reclaim
You give the shopkeeper 172 gold and reclaim the white colored sword.
