MP cost: 10
School:  Illusion
Domain:  Trickery

The blur spell blurs the outline of the target, making them more difficult
to hit in combat. The caster of this spell does not suffer this penalty
when attacking the target. The true-sight spell also allows the attacker
to see through the blur effect.

Syntax:
cast blur <target>
