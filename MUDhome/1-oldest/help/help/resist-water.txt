MP cost: 13
School:  Abjuration
Domain:  Protection

This spell halves all magical spell damage from the water realm. An intangible
shield of water forms around the target, attempting to nullify and offensive
magic that comes toward it and the target. Note that only magic from the realm
of water is affected.

See "help resist" for more information.

Syntax:
cast resist-water <target>
