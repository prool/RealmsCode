MP cost: 15
School:  Necromancy
Domain:  Evil

This spell is a necromantic attack spell. When cast on an enemy it will
harm them with negative energy, healing the caster in the process. A lich
cannot be healed in this manner - instead, their damage is increased.

Syntax:
cast siphon-life <target>
