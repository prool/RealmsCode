MP cost: 15
School:  Illusion
Domain:  Trickery

This spell turns its target invisible. Normal means of sight, including
infravision, will not be able to detect the target. However, those under the
influence of detect-invisibility will be able to see the target. Note that any
offensive actions performed while under this spell will cause it to wear off.

Syntax:
cast invis <target>
