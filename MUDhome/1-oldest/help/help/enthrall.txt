Clerics of Aramon are so commanding over undead, that they are able to enthrall
the creatures into not attacking them. It is similar in effect to bard charm or
vampire hypnotize, except that it only works on undead players or undead monsters.

Example:
: enth gh
You enthrall the ghoul with the power of Aramon.
