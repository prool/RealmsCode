The Realms Forum is located here: ^Whttp://forums.rohonline.net/^x

The forum command will allow you to associate your character with a forum
account, or visit the following URL for more information about association:
^Whttp://www.rohonline.net/assoc.php^x

Syntax:
To unassociate this character from this forum account type:
   forum ^Cremove^x
To associate this character with a forum account type:
   forum ^C<forum account> <forum password>^x
