MP cost: Dependant on the number of targets.
School:  Evocation
Domain:  Destruction

This spell affects all monsters in the area that the spell is cast in. A
blazing inferno ignites and incinerates the targets of this spell. It is part
of the fire realm.

Type "help aoe" for more information.

Syntax:
cast inferno [<monsters>|<players>|<all>]
