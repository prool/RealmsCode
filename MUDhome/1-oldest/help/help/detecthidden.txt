MP cost: 5
School:  Divination
Domain:  Knowledge

The detect-hidden spell allows the caster to momentarily see hidden
creatures, objects, and exits in a room. A resist-magic spell may protect a
hidden creature from being revealed by this spell.

Syntax:
cast detect-hidden
