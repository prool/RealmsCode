Help on DM command:
	DMFINGER

Dmfinger displays the info page for a player, whether they are logged
on or not. It also displays the normal 'finger' information, as well
as what room they are in.

: dmfinger min
-Min's info page-
Room Number: 123
Currently logged on.
No unread mail since: Wed Jul 31 17:54:05 2002
