Help on: *status

This command allows a builder or higher view the current
stats of any given room, object, monster, or player. It
will tell you all flags set, combat stats in case of a
monster or weapon, as well as traps, exit flags, etc for
any given room.

Syntax:
To *stat a room:        *stat
To *stat an object:     *stat (object)
To *stat a monster:     *stat (monster)
To *stat a player:      *stat (player)
