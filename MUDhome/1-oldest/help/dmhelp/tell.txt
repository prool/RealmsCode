Help on DM command:
	TELL

Tell flashes the exact string specified to the player.

: *tell min The wino attacks you.
You flashed: "The wino attacks you." to Min.
: *tell min ### Bob just flashed, "I'm going to kill you!".
You flashed: "### Bob just flashed, "I'm going to kill you!"." to Min.

(23 H 5 M): The wino attacks you.
(23 H 5 M): ### Bob just flashed, "I'm going to kill you!".
