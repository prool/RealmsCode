Help on: *take

This command allows DMs and CTs to remotely remove items from
players' inventories or from containers in players' inventories.
The items are added to the inventory of the DM or CT.

examples:
:*take blacke goatboy
:You remove a blackened-steel eviscerator(+2)from Goatboy's inventory.
:*take hazy mastemage bag
:You remove a hazy potion from Mastemage's buttercup bag.
:*take ada genie bag 3
:You remove some adamantine corbite armor from Genie's senna bag.
:*take leaf 4 fawn indigo 5
:You remove a yew leaf from Fawn's indigo bag.
