Help on DM command:
	REPAIR

Repair will fix the target's armor and weapons, setting their
shots back to the maximum for the item. Note that this only
fixes items the target is wearing / wielding, not all items in
their inventory. ALL items held / worn will be repaired.

: *repair min
Min's cloth pants repaired to full shots (10/10).
Min's wooden shield repaired to full shots (10/10).
Min's hoe repaired to full shots (4/4).
Min's empyrean wand repaired to full shots (2/2).
### Dominus repaired all of Min's armor.

: *repair min
Min is wearing nothing that needs to be repaired.
