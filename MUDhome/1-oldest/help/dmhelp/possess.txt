Help on DM command:
	POSSESS

Allows the DM to possess a monster and act as that monster. When
possessed, a monsters flag FollowDM(47) is set. Anyone who attacks
and is killed by the possessed monster does not lose experience.
Any social commands by the DM appear as if by the monster.

: *pos wino
You possess a wino.
(9 H 0 M): broad Hahaha!
### A wino broadcasted, "Hahaha!"
(9 H 0 M): *pos wino
You release a wino's body.
