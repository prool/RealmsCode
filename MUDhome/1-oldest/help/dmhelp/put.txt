Help on: *put

This command allows DMs to remotely add items to a player's
inventory or to the containers in a player's inventory.
The items must be in the inventory of the DM.

examples:
:*put blacke goatboy
:You add the blackened-steel eviscerator(+2)to Goatboy's inventory.
:*put hazy mastemage bag
:You add the hazy potion to Mastemage's buttercup bag.
:*put ada genie bag 3
:You add some adamantine corbite armor to Genie's senna bag.
:*put leaf 4 fawn indigo 5
:You add the yew leaf to Fawn's indigo bag.
